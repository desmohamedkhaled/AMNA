Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day delivery in Cairo for orders placed before 2 PM, and 2-3 business days for nationwide delivery across Egypt.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I return products?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Returns are free for defective items.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within Egypt. We're working on expanding our delivery network to other countries.

How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email once your order is processed. You can also track your order in your dashboard.

What if my order is damaged?

If your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! Our customer support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, and live chat to help with any questions or concerns.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, orders are processed and cannot be changed.

Are your products authentic?

Absolutely! We only sell authentic, brand-new products from authorized suppliers. All items come with manufacturer warranties.

How do I create an account?

Click on "Sign Up" in the top navigation, fill out the registration form with your details, and verify your email address.

Still have questions?

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
